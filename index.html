About Us

Established in June of 2010 from an idea to help keep Young Males out of trouble, this is the ONLY Enrichment Camp for boys in Stark County and the surrounding areas. The Governor’s office recognized JustUs Boyz, as the only Grass Roots Enrichment camp in the State of Ohio filling the needs of boys aging from 10 through 18 years old covering areas such as: Bullying, Future Careers, Importance of Education, Drugs and many more topics that youth are facing in today’s society. JustUs Boyz were also chosen as the Educational Community Activist Award in 2017 by the Canton City Schools District Teachers Union.

Our Mission

The Mission of the camp is to invite young men aging 10-18 years old, into an environment that will Foster their development of POSITIVE Goals. These goals will help them in their decision making and accountability when making Life Choices.

Our Founder

Elder Toriano D. Jones

Founder/Facilitator

As a Man of God, a former Educator and presently a Correction Officer I believe that it's my job to help build Boyz. Each child is worthy of being Role models /or Mentors as they enter into Manhood. Developing the Boyz into young men of Character, Integrity, & Courage is my Goal! Young men with both Confidence & Humility. The young men that attend JustUs Boyz Enrichment Camp will know the Value of Family & Faith as well as having a Career. Sports is not our focus, however, I use several sporting events to build relationships with our young men. Sports such as Basketball, Fishing, Laser tag, Bowling, & Swimming ect...
